AK-47

The AK-47 is a powerful assault rifle with high damage. Great for taking out zombies quickly.

Best Loadout:

  1. Attachments: Quickdraw, Grip, Extended Mags
  2. Perks: Juggernog, Speed Cola, Stamin-Up
  3. Equipment: Monkey Bombs

Ray Gun

The Ray Gun is a classic weapon that deals high damage to zombies. Ideal for crowd control.

Best Loadout:

  1. Attachments: N/A (special weapon)
  2. Perks: Juggernog, Double Tap, Quick Revive
  3. Equipment: Gersch Device

VMP

The VMP is an SMG with a fast fire rate, perfect for close quarters.

Best Loadout:

  1. Attachments: Rapid Fire, Extended Mags
  2. Perks: Juggernog, Speed Cola
  3. Equipment: Monkey Bombs

Brecci

The Brecci is a shotgun with high damage, excellent for close-range encounters.

Best Loadout:

  1. Attachments: Long Barrel, Rapid Fire
  2. Perks: Juggernog, Stamin-Up
  3. Equipment: C4

Haymaker 12

The Haymaker 12 is another powerful shotgun that can clear hordes of zombies.

Best Loadout:

  1. Attachments: Long Barrel, Fast Mags
  2. Perks: Juggernog, Quick Revive
  3. Equipment: Gersch Device
